description: 'Genius loci is a personal project created for the collective exhibition Le génie du lieu, in a former pharmacy that has been transformed into an art space. The work interrogates the environment, focusing on the irregularities of the architecture and on the traces of the space’s past life. The intervention analyses and catalogues these traces, reproducing them almost imperceptibly in pencil and gouache drawings on certain walls.'
